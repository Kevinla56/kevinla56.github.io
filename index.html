OMOK

OMOK is a 2 player board game that combines the concept of tik-tac-toe and connect 4 to make a game designed that allows the player to place their pieces anywhere within the board, first to 5 wins. Created using Java Swing, players are able to compete with eachother locally until one prevails or ends up in a draw.
